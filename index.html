Nana Sørensen

Scenario:

Nana Sørensen is Math teacher in primary school. She wants to make learning fun. She would like to use games in teaching, but she also wants that all kids learn and play together. She heard about Weco Play from her colleagues, so she goes online in search for the company and the games.

EXPECTATIONS:
  • "I want to make Math fun."
  • "I want everyone to participate in activity"

Stages

Doing

Thinking

Saying

Stage 1
AWARENESS

Hears about colleagues about learning games she could use in the classroom. Start search online.
Nothing comes up, at least nothing similar to what my colleagues described. I cannot think of any other words to use. What is the name of the company again?

"Maybe I should ask my colleagues again, let me try to contact them on Facebook"

Stage 2
CONSIDERING OPTIONS

Searches in Google, uses company´s name this time.
I cannot see the games, would this even work for me? I wish I can see how it works. Where is the price?
"No YouTube link? Maybe I should check other options. Wait, here is the demo. Should I check on Facebook?"

Stage 3
DECISION

After seeing videos on Facebook, and one video on YouTube decides to go to principal to ask if school would consider buying the product.
This would be great addition for curricullum for everyone. It is a package of games, we could all benefit from it.
"I want our school to buy this product."

Stage 4
POST-EXPERIENCE

Goes into teachers groups on social media and recommends product.
I wish more people are aware of this product. I even got one feature in a game by personal request. What a great support!
"Finaly, the students are engaged and seem to enjoy Maths."

Insights

  • Nana had a difficulty finding product online
  • Once she found the website, she was confused with the informations
  • She missed visual representation of the product
  • She invested lots of time to get information that persuaded her to make purchase

Internal ownership

  • Better SEO
  • Better visual representation with focus on value
  • Connection between platforms: website should link to social media and vice-versa
